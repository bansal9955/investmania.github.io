About bitcoin

Bitcoin is the world’s most valuable currency; today, it is even more valuable than gold. The rate of this cryptocurrency has been growing exponentially. Many people are buying Bitcoin as an investment or use it to buy products and services.
How Does Bitcoin Work? Bitcoin operates without a central authority or banks due to peer-to-peer technology; the network manages transactions and issues bitcoins collectively. Bitcoin is open-source, meaning it is owned by no one or manages, and anybody can participate. Bitcoin's many unique properties allow it to be used in ways that no other payment mechanism has been able to. A bitcoin wallet has two keys: a public key and a private key. These keys function together to allow the owner to initiate and digitally sign transactions, proving authorization.
